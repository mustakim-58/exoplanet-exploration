Exomos

EXOMOS is a dynamic group of space enthusiasts dedicated to simplifying exoplanet exploration and making it accessible to everyone. Led by Sajedul Hossain Nahin, a passionate developer and researcher, the team brings together a diverse range of skills and expertise. Mrinmoy Chakraborty Argho, a talented researcher and graphic designer, contributes to the team's creative vision. Mustakim Billah, a brilliant web developer, ensures the team's website is user-friendly and informative. Mohammod Samiur Rahman Sohan, a dedicated field researcher, provides valuable insights from the ground. And our intellectual team advisor offers guidance and support throughout the project. Together, Team EXOMOS is united by a shared passion for space exploration and a commitment to making complex scientific concepts understandable to all.
